About KidsBook

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Who We Are

KidsBook is a fun, interactive learning app designed to help children build a strong foundation in early education. We believe that learning should be joyful, engaging, and accessible to every child.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make learning fun and effective for children aged 3–8. We combine colorful visuals, interactive activities, and educational content to create an engaging learning experience that kids love.

What We Offer

KidsBook provides a comprehensive learning experience including:

  • ABCD Learning – English alphabet with fun images
  • A for Apple – Vocabulary building with pictures
  • Hindi Learning – Hindi alphabet and Varnmala
  • Numbers & Counting – Learn 1 to 100 with fun activities
  • Multiplication Tables – Practice tables with ease
  • Stories & Songs – Engaging audio and video content

Our Values

  • Child Safety First We prioritize children's safety and privacy. Our app complies with COPPA and Google Play's Family Policy.
  • Quality Education All content is carefully curated by educators to ensure quality learning.
  • Fun Learning We believe children learn best when they're having fun.
  • Accessibility We strive to make learning accessible to every child.

Meet Our Team

We are a passionate team of educators, developers, and designers dedicated to creating the best learning experience for children.

Common Questions

  • 1. Is KidsBook safe for my child? Yes! KidsBook is designed with child safety as our top priority. We comply with COPPA regulations and Google Play's Family Policy. We do not collect personal information from children.
  • 2. Is KidsBook free? Yes, KidsBook is completely free to download and use. We show ads to keep the app free for everyone.
  • 3. What age group is KidsBook for? KidsBook is designed for children aged 3–8 years old.
  • 4. Can I use KidsBook offline? Some features require internet connectivity for ads and content updates. We recommend using the app with an internet connection.
  • 5. How do I report an issue? If you encounter any issues, please email us at ksarvesh878@gmail.com with details about the problem.
  • 6. How can I support the app? Share KidsBook with friends and family! Your support helps us create more educational content.
