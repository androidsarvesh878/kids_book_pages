Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KidsBook

General Questions

  • What is KidsBook? KidsBook is an educational learning app for children that teaches ABCD, numbers, Hindi, tables, and more through fun interactive activities.
  • Is KidsBook free? Yes! KidsBook is completely free to download and use. We may show ads to keep the app free.
  • What age is KidsBook for? KidsBook is designed for children aged 3–8 years old.
  • Is KidsBook safe for kids? Absolutely! We comply with COPPA and Google Play's Family Policy. We do not collect personal information from children.

Learning Questions

  • What subjects does KidsBook cover? KidsBook covers English alphabet (ABCD), vocabulary (A for Apple), Hindi alphabet (Varnmala), numbers (1–100), multiplication tables, stories, and songs.
  • How does KidsBook help my child learn? KidsBook uses colorful visuals, audio pronunciation, interactive activities, and fun games to make learning engaging and effective.
  • Does KidsBook have audio support? Yes! Most learning modules include audio pronunciation to help children learn correct pronunciation.

Technical Questions

  • Does KidsBook work offline? Some features work offline, but internet connectivity is recommended for ads and content updates.
  • How do I update the app? Updates are available through Google Play Store. We recommend enabling automatic updates.
  • What permissions does KidsBook need? KidsBook only requires basic permissions needed for app functionality.

Ad Questions

  • Why does KidsBook show ads? Ads help us keep KidsBook free for everyone. All ads are child-appropriate and comply with Google Play's Family Policy.
  • Can I remove ads? Currently, there is no ad-free version available.
